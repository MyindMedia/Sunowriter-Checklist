# Lesson 03 — Your First Login & Account Setup

**Duration:** 4 minutes
**Outcome:** You are logged in, your default mode is set, and Help tooltips are turned on.
**Prerequisites:** Active Myind Sound Music Club membership.

---

## Context (for the LMS module page)

Access to Suno Writer is gated through your Myind Sound community membership. When you joined, your email was added to the access list automatically. This lesson walks you through your first login, accepts the terms, and sets the two preferences I want every new member to set on day one.

## What You Will Learn
- How invites work behind the scenes
- How to log in for the first time
- How to set your default mode
- How to turn Help tooltips on
- What to do if you cannot get in

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open the Suno Writer login URL.
2. Enter the email tied to your Myind Sound Music Club membership.
3. Check your inbox for the magic link or one-time code and click it.
4. Accept the terms on first login.
5. Click your User Profile in the bottom-left of the sidebar.
6. Set Default Mode to Songwriting (or whichever mode you use most).
7. Click the Help toggle in the top-right of the main panel and turn it ON.
8. Close the profile panel. You are ready.

## Screen Recording Script

### [00:00 — 00:20] Cold open

**[ON SCREEN]** Suno Writer login page.

**Narration:**
"Four minutes to get you logged in and set up the right way. Let's go."

### [00:20 — 01:00] How access works

**[ON SCREEN]** Login form on screen.

**Narration:**
"Quick context. Access is tied to your Myind Sound Music Club membership. When you joined, your email was added automatically. You do not create a password. You enter your email, you get a magic link, you click it, you are in. If you ever lose access we can fix it in under a minute."

### [01:00 — 02:00] Logging in

**[ON SCREEN]** Type email, click submit, switch to inbox, click magic link, return to app.

**Narration:**
"Type the email you used to join the community. Hit submit. Check your inbox. Click the link. You are in. First time only, you will see a terms of service screen. Read it, accept it. Now you are sitting in the dashboard."

### [02:00 — 03:00] Setting your default mode

**[ON SCREEN]** Click User Profile in sidebar. Open Default Mode dropdown.

**Narration:**
"Now the two settings every new member should set on day one. Open your User Profile down here in the sidebar. Find Default Mode. Pick the mode you use most. For most of you that is Songwriting. This means every time you open the app you land in your favorite mode instead of guessing."

### [03:00 — 03:40] Turning Help on

**[ON SCREEN]** Top right of main panel. Click Help toggle. Hover over a button to show a tooltip firing.

**Narration:**
"Now turn Help tooltips ON. Top right of the main panel. Toggle this for your first week. Now when you hover over any button, a tooltip explains exactly what it does. After a week you will know it cold and you can turn it back off."

### [03:40 — 04:00] If you cannot get in

**[ON SCREEN]** Face cam.

**Narration:**
"If your link does not arrive, check spam, then post in the community help channel. We will fix it fast. Next lesson, we build your first full song. See you there."
