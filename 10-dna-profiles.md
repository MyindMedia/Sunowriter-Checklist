# Lesson 10 — DNA Profiles (Save, Match, Blend)

**Duration:** 12 minutes
**Outcome:** You save your sonic DNA, match it against others, and blend two profiles into a new direction.
**Prerequisites:** Lesson 07 (Sample Analysis) complete.

---

## Context (for the LMS module page)

DNA Profiles are the secret to staying consistent across releases. Every time you analyze a track or generate a song you love, you can save that result as a DNA Profile. The profile captures the sonic fingerprint, not the song itself, so you can reuse it as creative direction forever. Match lets you find your closest sonic neighbor in your library. Blend lets you merge two profiles into a brand new direction. This is how you build a signature sound on purpose instead of by accident.

## What You Will Learn
- What a DNA Profile actually contains
- How to save a profile from a track you love
- How to use Match to find your closest sonic neighbor
- How to use Blend to merge two profiles
- How to generate from a blended DNA Profile
- Why this is the secret weapon for consistent releases

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open Sample Analysis Mode and analyze a track you love.
2. At the bottom of the analysis, click Save as DNA Profile.
3. Name it. Example: "Late Night R&B Vibe v1".
4. Repeat with a second track to create a second profile.
5. Open the DNA panel from the sidebar.
6. Click Match on profile one. The app shows the closest sonic neighbors in your library.
7. Click Blend. Pick two profiles. Hit Blend.
8. The app returns a new merged profile.
9. Generate a song from the blended profile by clicking Use in Songwriting Mode.
