# Module 01 — Suno Writer by Myind Sound

**Author:** Lawrence "ThaMyind" Berment
**Audience:** Myind Sound Music Club community members
**Module Goal:** Take a member from first login to shipping a finished Suno track using all four modes of Suno Writer.

## Brand Standard for Every Lesson Asset
- Background: `#1A1A1A`
- Headlines: Inter Black, gold `#FDB913`
- Body: white `#FFFFFF`
- Accents and dividers: orange `#FF8C00`
- No em dashes anywhere in voiceover, slides, or captions
- Voice: direct, warm, peer to peer, "you" often, no hype

## Hard Rules Honored in Every Lesson
- No em dashes in any output
- No artist names anywhere — translate to sonic descriptions
- No background or backing vocals unless explicitly requested
- Song length target 2:30 to 3:30
- Preferred song structure: Intro → Hook → Verse 1 → Hook → Verse 2 → Bridge → Hook → Outro
- Cliché blacklist: "in my chest", "pressure makes me fold", "flame", "light", "storm", "scars", "manifest" (unless theological flip)
- Intro is always instrumental unless requested otherwise
- Bridge always modulates with a key change
- Style and Lyric prompts live in separate copy blocks with labels OUTSIDE the copy window
