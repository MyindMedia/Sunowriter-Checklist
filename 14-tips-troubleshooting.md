# Lesson 14 — Tips, Shortcuts & Troubleshooting

**Duration:** 8 minutes
**Outcome:** You avoid the top ten mistakes new users make and know exactly what to do when something goes wrong.
**Prerequisites:** Lessons 01 to 13 complete.

---

## Context (for the LMS module page)

Every new tool has the same problem. The first week feels magical, then you hit a wall. Suno Writer is no exception. This lesson is the wall avoided. Ten mistakes I see people make, the fixes for each, and the troubleshooting steps for the three things that go wrong most often.

## What You Will Learn
- The top ten mistakes new users make
- The fix for each mistake
- How to handle login issues, audio upload limits, and browser quirks
- When to ask for help in the community

---

## Top 10 Mistakes & Fixes

1. **Writing vague prompts** — fix: name the genre, the mood, the setting, and the energy in one sentence.
2. **Cranking Complexity to 100 by default** — fix: start at 50 and only push higher when the song needs to feel like an event.
3. **Letting clichés through** — fix: trust the blacklist, but if you spot one, type "rewrite without the cliché in slide three" and the app fixes it.
4. **Ignoring the H-Pads** — fix: use them. They are the fastest way to shape texture without rewriting the prompt.
5. **Mixing Style and Lyric prompts in one paste** — fix: always paste them in their separate Suno fields. Suno wants them apart.
6. **Forgetting to upload a reference track for cohesion across an EP** — fix: upload the lead single as silent reference for every other track on the project.
7. **Ignoring DNA Profiles** — fix: save three by the end of your first week. Match and Blend become powerful at three plus.
8. **Skipping Arrange View** — fix: use it for any song that has more than two sections you care about.
9. **Releasing without Release Prep** — fix: run Release Prep on every track before you upload to your distribution platform.
10. **Working in silence** — fix: post your work in the community. The feedback loop is the entire point.

## Top 3 Troubleshooting Issues

1. **Cannot log in** — check spam for the magic link. If it is not there within five minutes, post in the community help channel.
2. **Audio upload fails** — check the file size (max 25 MB) and the format (MP3 or WAV). Re-export and try again.
3. **Browser quirks** — clear cache, try Chrome or Brave. If a button does not respond, refresh the page. Your work is auto-saved.

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Read the top ten list above.
2. Identify the two mistakes you are most likely to make.
3. Set a reminder for yourself to avoid them on your next session.
4. Bookmark the troubleshooting section.
