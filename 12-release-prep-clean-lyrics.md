# Lesson 12 — Release Prep & Clean Lyrics

**Duration:** 8 minutes
**Outcome:** You take a finished song through Release Prep and generate a clean version of explicit lyrics.
**Prerequisites:** A finished song from Lesson 04 saved in your library.

---

## Context (for the LMS module page)

A song is not done when Suno renders it. It is done when it is ready to ship. Release Prep gives you a release-ready checklist generated specifically for the song you wrote. Clean Lyrics takes any explicit lyric set and gives you a radio-friendly version with the same energy. Both are built for the moment when you stop being a songwriter and become a release artist.

## What You Will Learn
- How to run Release Prep on any saved work
- What Release Prep generates and how to use it
- How to convert explicit lyrics into a clean version
- How to rate a generation so the app learns your taste over time

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open Saved Works and pick a finished song.
2. Click Release Prep.
3. Read the generated checklist. It includes title suggestions, metadata fields, hook line for marketing, and platform-ready descriptions.
4. Copy the parts you need into your distribution platform.
5. To clean lyrics, open the lyric prompt for any saved work.
6. Click Clean Lyrics.
7. The app rewrites the lyrics with radio-safe language while keeping the same flow and meaning.
8. Copy the clean version into a new Suno generation if you need a clean release.
9. Rate any generation in the app. Your ratings feed your Taste Profile so future outputs get sharper.

**[ON SCREEN]** Click Release Prep button. Watch the checklist generate.

"Open any finished song from your Saved Works. Click Release Prep. Watch. The app generates a release checklist made for this specific song. Title suggestions in case you do not have one yet. **[ON SCREEN]** Face cam.

**Narration:**
"Most independent artists release a song and then freeze on the metadata. They do not know what to call it. They do not know what to write in the description. ### [04:00 — 06:00] Clean Lyrics

**[ON SCREEN]** Open the lyrics for an explicit song. Click Clean Lyrics. Watch the rewrite.

**Narration:**
"Clean Lyrics. Open the lyric prompt for any saved work. Click Clean Lyrics. The app rewrites every explicit line with a radio-safe version that keeps the same flow and the same meaning. Notice it does not gut the song. It rewrites the words around the energy. Copy the clean version into a new Suno generation if you need to ship a clean release alongside the explicit one."

### [06:00 — 07:30] Rating and Taste Profile

**[ON SCREEN]** Click the rating buttons under a generation. Show the Taste Profile panel.

**Narration:**
"Last thing. Rate your generations. Every time you mark a result as a hit or a miss, the app updates your Taste Profile. Over time the engine biases its outputs toward what you have already approved. This is how the tool gets sharper the more you use it. Two seconds per rating. Massive payoff over a month."

### [07:30 — 08:00] Close
