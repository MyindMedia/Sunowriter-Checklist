## Section Energy Map (default values)

| Section | Energy (0–100) | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Intro | 20 | Instrumental only |
| Hook | 80 | Lands first to anchor the song |
| Verse 1 | 40 | Story setup |
| Hook | 85 | Slightly higher than first |
| Verse 2 | 45 | Story development |
| Bridge | 60 | Key change required |
| Hook | 90 | Highest energy hit |
| Outro | 25 | Fade or tape stop |

## The Hard Rules (verbatim text for the rules box)

- Hook hits early
- Bridge always modulates with a key change
- Intro is always instrumental unless explicitly requested
- Target song length is 2:30 to 3:30
- No background or backing vocals unless explicitly requested
- Section-by-section energy control is the goal
