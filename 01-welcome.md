# Lesson 01 — Welcome to Suno Writer

**Duration:** 5 minutes
**Outcome:** You know what Suno Writer is, who built it, and why it exists.
**Prerequisites:** None. Just hit play.

---

## Context (for the LMS module page)

Suno Writer is the Myind Sound community's in-house tool for getting Suno AI to actually sound the way you hear it in your head. It replaces the custom GPT Lawrence used privately for years and puts the same engine in your hands. In this lesson you will learn what the tool is, who it is built for, and what you will be able to do by the end of the module.

## What You Will Learn
- What Suno Writer is in one sentence
- The problem it solves
- Who it is built for
- The four modes you will master in this module
- What you can do by the end of Module 1

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open the lesson video.
2. Watch the full 5 minutes start to finish.
3. Open Suno Writer in a new tab and bookmark it.
4. Join the community channel where you will post your homework.
5. Move on to Lesson 02.

## Screen Recording Script

### [00:00 — 00:15] Cold open

**[ON SCREEN]** Suno Writer dashboard, dark background, logo visible.

**Narration:**
"If you have ever typed a prompt into Suno and got back something that sounds nothing like what you imagined, this whole module is for you. I built Suno Writer so the Myind Sound community never has to fight the AI again."

### [00:15 — 01:00] What it is

**[ON SCREEN]** Switch to face cam, studio background. Lower third overlay: "Lawrence 'ThaMyind' Berment".

**Narration:**
"Suno Writer is a web app I built for our community. It takes a simple idea from you and turns it into the exact two prompts Suno needs, one Style Prompt and one Lyric Prompt, structured the way real songs are structured, with no fluff and no clichés. It is the same tool I use on every release I put out."

### [01:00 — 02:00] Why it exists

**[ON SCREEN]** Quick screen-recording B-roll of typing a generic prompt into Suno and getting a generic result.

**Narration:**
"Here is the problem. Suno is powerful, but if you give it a vague prompt you get a vague song. Most producers waste hours guessing at the right words. I had a custom GPT solving this for me privately for over a year. The community kept asking for access. So I rebuilt it as a real app, hosted in the cloud, so every member of Myind Sound Music Club can use it the same way I do."

### [02:00 — 03:30] Who it is for

**[ON SCREEN]** Cut back to face cam.

**Narration:**
"This is for you if you make beats and want to flesh them into full songs. It is for you if you write songs and want to test ideas faster. It is for you if you produce for other artists and need to translate a vague vibe into a real direction. It does not matter if you have used Suno once or a thousand times. By the end of this module you will use it like a professional tool, not a slot machine."

### [03:30 — 04:30] The four modes preview

**[ON SCREEN]** Back to the app. Hover over each mode button slowly.

**Narration:**
"Suno Writer has four modes. Songwriting Mode for full songs with lyrics. Instrumental Mode for beats and remixes with no vocals. Sample Maker Mode for drumless loops you can chop. And Sample Analysis Mode where you upload any track and the app breaks it down and gives you a Suno prompt that recreates the vibe. We are going to walk through every single one."

### [04:30 — 05:00] Close

**[ON SCREEN]** Face cam. Lower third: "Next: Lesson 02 — The Interface Tour".

**Narration:**
"In the next lesson I am going to walk you around the interface so you know exactly where everything lives. Hit next and I will see you over there."
